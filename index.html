🐻 Did you know? Montana facts
🏔
Lone Peak towers at 11,166 feet right above Big Sky. On a clear day you can see it from 80 miles away.
🐻
Montana has the largest grizzly bear population in the lower 48. Make noise on the trails — they don't want to see you either.
🌌
Montana has some of the darkest night skies in the US. The Milky Way is genuinely visible with the naked eye. Put your phone down.
🎿
Big Sky Resort has 5,850 skiable acres — the most in America. Those same slopes make for epic summer hiking terrain.
🦌
Montana has more elk, deer & antelope than people. Keep your eyes open on every drive — wildlife sightings are basically guaranteed.
🌡
July highs average 77°F, but nights drop to 43°F. Yes you'll want a jacket for the rodeo. No, you won't regret it.
